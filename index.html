How to use this tool
A guide for navigating the Buendía genealogy

Selecting characters

Click any card to select it. Connecting lines appear between the selected character and all their relatives, color-coded by relationship type. Connected cards receive matching colored borders: gold = spouse, blue = parent, purple = child, pink = lover. Click the card again or press Esc to deselect.

Character notes (📄)

Click the 📄 icon on any card to open a detailed notes panel with three sections: Arc in the novel, Themes & significance, and What to watch for. Within the panel, click any relationship button to jump directly to that character's notes.

Name disambiguation (👥)

Click 👥 Name Twins in the toolbar to open the disambiguation panel. It groups all characters sharing a name — José Arcadio (4 characters), Aureliano (5 + a collective), Remedios (2), Amaranta (2) — showing their distinguishing traits side by side. Use View → to select any character on the canvas.

Connection filters

The Connections filters (Blood, Romantic, Descent, Unions) show all relationships of a given type across the full tree when nothing is selected, or filter visible connections when a card is selected.

Thematic filters

The Themes row (Solitude, War, Forbidden love, Fate & repetition, Magic, Vitality) dims all characters not associated with that theme, letting you trace a single motif across generations.

Navigating & rearranging

Drag the empty canvas to pan. Drag any card to reposition it — cards spring back toward home with collision avoidance when released. Click Reset positions to restore the original layout. Type in the Search box to filter by name, role, or badge.
